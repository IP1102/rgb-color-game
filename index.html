How to play?

  • The above image, showcases the colors produced as a result of the mixing of RED, GREEN, and BLUE.
  • A RGB color is described by it's RED, GREEN, and BLUE intensities.
  • The intensity varies between 0 - 255
  • Higher intensities of REDand GREEN produces a YELLOWISH color, higher intensities of GREEN, and BLUE produces CYAN and higher intensities of RED and BLUE produces VIOLET color.
  • If all the RED, GREEN, and BLUE channels are high, WHEATISH color is produced.

Time Trial Mode

  • The objective of the game is to guess the correct square having the color same as that displayed above in terms of RGB(r, g, b) intensities.
  • 9 pts is awarded for successful guess in the first try.
  • Each re-try costs you 1 pt.
  • You have 30 seconds of time and you get a maximum of 30 retries.
